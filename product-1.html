The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S Class S 500 comes with an aerodynamic sporty design - minor changes have been made to the exterior of the Mercedes S Class 2021 to enter the modern era

Mercedes S Class 2021 Exterior Design

The hood is longer
Headlights and wheels are more noticeable - 21-inch rims
The C-shaft flows more smoothly into the back shoulder.
For the first time in three generations, the 2021 Mercedes S-Class gets horizontal taillights.
The door handles are now completely overlapping with the body.

Mercedes S Class 2021 Dimensions and Measurements

In terms of size
the new S-class is up to 1.3 inches longer than its predecessor (in the long wheelbase model), up to 2.0 inches wider, and has a height of about 0.4 inches.
