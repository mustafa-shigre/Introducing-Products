Mercedes g63 2019

G63 2019 is the second and all-new generation of the famous Mercedes-AMG G63 cars, as the launch of the G-Class of Mercedes cars dates back to 1979, while the second generation was revealed in 2018 with mechanical and performance improvements and a more advanced cabin, but the German manufacturer could not change the design and the general shape remained the same, in what became known as the box-shaped design that highlights the exceptional capabilities of this distinctive car.

Mercedes g63 2019 Exterior Design

In terms of exterior, Mercedes has slightly increased the dimensions of the car, so the G36 2019 is taller, wider and taller than before.
We also see in the front the new grille with the design of AMG cars, and the front and rear lights and turn lights got LED lights, while the car is available with 20, 21 or 22-inch wheels

Mercedes g63 2019 Dimensions and Measurements

The car was equipped with a 4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, instead of the 5.5-liter engine in the previous generation, generating 577 hp and 850 Nm, and this engine got several updates compared to the previous engine where the pistons were developed, the turbo cooling process was improved and newer programming was used.

Mercedes g63 2019 Interior

The Mercedes G63 2019 got a comprehensive update from the interior, with touches inspired by the exterior design, such as air conditioning vents similar to circular headlights and control buttons similar to turn lights, and we also see the traditional handle in front of the front passenger, which is one of the distinctive fingerprints of the interior of the G-Class.

Mercedes g63 2019 Prices

In the United Arab Emirates, the price of the Mercedes G 63 2019 is about 720 thousand dirhams, equivalent to 196 thousand US dollars or 735 thousand Saudi riyals.
